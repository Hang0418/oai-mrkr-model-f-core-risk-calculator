Model Information

Development cohorts, intended populations, validation statistics, and target-cohort recalibration.

Early model

CHECK

Population: symptomatic baseline KL0/1 knees.

Outcome: 24-month incident KL >=2.

Model: penalized logistic regression.

AUC: 0.786. Brier: 0.089.

Arthroplasty model

OAI

Population: 24-month landmark knees, KL0-4.

Outcome: subsequent 60-month target-knee TKA/KR.

Model: penalized logistic regression.

AUC: 0.789. Brier: 0.089.

Transport evidence

MRKR

Role: real-world transport and recalibration dataset.

AUC: 0.700 at 24 months; 0.723 at 36 months.

Finding: ranking was retained, but absolute risk required target-horizon recalibration.

About

This calculator is a research-use implementation of stage-specific knee OA prediction models. Outputs are not validated treatment thresholds and should not be used to determine surgical eligibility.

The early module estimates 24-month incident definite radiographic OA in symptomatic CHECK knees with baseline KL0/1. The arthroplasty module estimates 60-month target-knee TKA/KR risk after a 24-month OAI landmark. MRKR evidence is presented as transport and recalibration information for researchers.
